Rules

As of 2020, there isn’t a single, unified ruleset for playing jugger across the world. There are around 20 different national and local variants, most of which agree on the basics but differ in certain areas like refereeing or the exact measures of the spars.

We’re currently working on creating a comparative database for the German, Spanish and Australian rules - in the meantime, check out the International Jugger Blog’s Rulebooks compilation!

Find a Club

Jugger can be played almost anywhere!

Currently, there are jugger clubs in thirty countries across Europe, Asia, Oceania and the Americas. Check our Jugger World Map to see if there’s any such group near you - but if there isn’t, grab your friends and start one!
