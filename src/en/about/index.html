Mission Statement

We want to know where Jugger is played, how it is played, and what they need from us to grow.

We want to gather experts and volunteers from around the world to provide all the material and agreements needed to support that growth.

We want to make it easier to compete with other Juggers from around the world.

This IJC Mission Statement was adopted on 2nd October, 2019 as part of its first Charter:

IJC Charter

Code of Conduct

The purpose of this Code of Conduct is to outline the International Jugger Council’s (IJC) commitment to an environment free from inappropriate behaviour, specifically Bullying, Violence, Harassment, Discrimination, Sexual Harassment and Victimisation, and provide a clear and simple incident and complaint handling procedure.

Do you coach other Juggers, promote your club locally, organize tournaments, or otherwise hold a position of leadership or seniority in your local Jugger organization? The IJC Code of Conduct provides a useful starting point to help you create an safe, enjoyable and inclusive environment for the sport of Jugger.

Who We Are

The IJC is a volunteer-run organization, run by Juggers who take an interest in supporting and growing the sport of Jugger. If you're interested in learning more, or want to help as a volunteer, you can contact us on Discord or via email:

  • Discord: Jugger Worldwide
  • juggerinternational@gmail.com

Support Us!

There are no membership fees - the IJC is a non-profit forum and is funded via individual donations. If you like the objectives we’re working to achieve, please consider supporting us by sending a small amount of money to our account! To get the bank details, just get in touch with the Treasurer via Discord or email.

IJC Committees

These committees help the IJC realize its mission. If there's a particular committee or area you'd like to help out with, you can also reach out to the relevant Commissioner on Discord!

Committee Commissioner What They Do
Web Presence Valkyrie Savage This team is in charge of designing and maintaining our website and online presence.
Corporate Image Valkyrie Savage This group focuses on designing and applying a coherent brand image for the IJC.
Mapping Gil Richard This section keeps track of the locations of the Jugger groups across the globe and updates the Jugger World Map.
Growth Support Gabriel Maynard Hernández This Committee elaborates guides and texts to advise newly founded clubs on how to improve their training, materiel building and advertising techniques.
Rules Comparison Wendy J-M This team is working on comparing all the existing Jugger rulebooks, thus setting the groundwork for their eventual unification into a single ruleset.
International Competition Ian Williams This group works to enable the establishment of new international Jugger tournaments, and is currently elaborating a guide on how to organize them at different scales.
Legal Support Pijus “Pope” Stankevičius This section is composing the necessary regulations and by-laws for the creation of the IJC as a solid institution that can help out Juggers at every level.
Ethics Miguel Alvarado This is a special committee, dedicated to study complaints of breaches of our Code of Conduct and resolve the cases through recommendations.
